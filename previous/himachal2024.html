Day 1: Arrival in Shimla

We arrived in Shimla, the queen of hills. After checking in at the hotel, we visited the famous Mall Road and had a relaxing evening.

Mall Road: A bustling street lined with shops, cafes, and colonial-style buildings, offering great views of the surrounding hills.
